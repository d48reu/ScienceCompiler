Source: PMID 33785315
Title: Sodium butyrate ameliorates the impairment of synaptic plasticity by inhibiting the neuroinflammation in 5XFAD mice
URL: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/33785315/

Study summary:
This early-stage Alzheimer's 5XFAD mouse study reported that sodium butyrate suppressed microglia-induced neuroinflammation and improved synaptic plasticity before major cognitive symptoms were obvious. Sodium butyrate decreased TNF-α, IL-6, and IL-1β, reduced microglial overactivation, and lowered Aβ accumulation. It also restored dendritic spine abundance and synapse-related proteins such as PSD-95, synaptophysin, and NR2B. The paper presents butyrate as an HDAC inhibitor that interrupts the inflammation–amyloid vicious cycle.
