Source: PMID 33832488
Title: Mild exacerbation of obesity- and age-dependent liver disease progression by senolytic cocktail dasatinib + quercetin
URL: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/33832488/

Study summary:
This obesity- and age-dependent NAFLD/HCC mouse study reported that the senolytic cocktail dasatinib plus quercetin did not remove senescent cells effectively in this model and slightly worsened liver disease progression and tumorigenesis. Histologic liver damage increased modestly and the treatment did not rescue major metabolic or fibrosis-related measures. The paper argues that dasatinib plus quercetin can be ineffective or mildly harmful in established obesity- and age-dependent liver disease contexts.
