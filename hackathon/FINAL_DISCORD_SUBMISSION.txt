One-line summary:
Science Compiler turns scientific literature into structured claims, contradiction maps, weighted evidence, and experiment ideas.

What it does:
- extracts/refines claims from paper text
- canonicalizes interventions/outcomes
- weights evidence
- surfaces contradiction or tradeoff structure
- proposes follow-up experiments
- supports human review instead of pretending to replace it

Best demo result:
In a butyrate/neuroinflammation corpus, it found a real context-dependent sign flip: mostly anti-neuroinflammatory across several non-parkinsonian contexts, but a parkinsonian MPTP paper points the other way.

Generalization:
In a second domain, senolytics/aging, it surfaced a different scientific structure: benefit-vs-harm tradeoffs across agents like D+Q, fisetin, and navitoclax.
