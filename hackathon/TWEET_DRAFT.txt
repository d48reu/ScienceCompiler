Built "Science Compiler" for the @NousResearch hackathon.

Instead of summarizing papers into mush, it compiles literature into:
- structured claims
- contradiction maps
- weighted evidence balances
- experiment proposals

In a butyrate/neuroinflammation corpus, it surfaced a real context-dependent sign flip:
mostly anti-neuroinflammatory across several contexts, but a parkinsonian MPTP paper points the other way.

Then we ran it on senolytics/aging and got a different pattern: not one contradiction, but a benefit-vs-harm tradeoff map across agents.

AI as a scientific instrument, not just a chatbot.
